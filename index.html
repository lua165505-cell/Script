FAQ

Built for legitimate creator workflows.

Does ScriptVault replace source control?

No. It complements Git-based workflows by focusing on secure delivery, release channels, and operational visibility.

Can teams manage multiple experiences?

Yes. Organize projects by game, environment, or studio team and control which integrations can access each release.

Is this meant for exploit distribution?

No. The platform is positioned for approved creator tooling, internal utilities, and secure module delivery inside authorized Roblox projects.
